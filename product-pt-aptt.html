Blue Cap PT APTT Tube

Size: 13x75 mm (1.8ml)

Packing: 100 tubes/pack, 12 packs/carton

Price: Rs. 16.00

Function:

Sodium Citrate acts as an anticoagulant by binding calcium in the blood. This tube is designed specifically for coagulation tests like PT (Prothrombin Time) and APTT (Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time).

Use:

Used in clinical laboratories for precise coagulation analysis. The 9:1 blood-to-anticoagulant ratio ensures reliable results.
