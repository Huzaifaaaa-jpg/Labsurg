EDTA K2 Tube

Description

EDTA K2 tubes are used for hematology tests such as CBC. They contain an anticoagulant (EDTA K2) which prevents clotting of blood, preserving the sample for accurate testing.

Usage

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Hemoglobin and WBC counts
  • ESR and Platelet studies
